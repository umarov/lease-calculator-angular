Lease Calculator.

Leasing a car has been pretty popular these days. It's affordable and easy. It mostly benefits the financial company that gives you the lease, but it also allows you easy access to a car. Well when you lease you have to be carefully knowing your limitations. This app focuses on one of them: The Mileage limit. When you sign a lease agreement, the dealership will let you know the number of miles you can drive per year. You choose this limit and it will affect your monthly payments. So what happens when you go over the limit? You will be presented with a bill of your overages when you return the car at the end of the lease. To avoid those charges, you can use this app to see how you are doing. ;)
